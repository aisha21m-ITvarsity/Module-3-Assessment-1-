Strawberry Milkshake

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 4

INGREDIENTS:




STEPS:
  1. Blend 4 ounces of strawberries, ¼ tsp. of vanilla, 1 cup of ice cream, and 2 tbsp. of milk in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour into 2 glasses. Repeat with the remaining strawberries, vanilla, ice cream and milk.
  3. Top with canned fresh cream and serve immediately.
