Cookies

Prep time: 30 mins

Yield: 5½ dozens

INGREDIENTS:









STEPS:
  1. Cream butter in a large mixing bowl; gradually add sugar, beat mixture until it is light and fluffy.
  2. Add your eggs, one at a time, beat it well after each addition.
  3. Add the coffee and fold it into the mixture well.
  4. Combine flour, soda, cinnamon, and allspice in a large mixing bowl. Stir in the raisins and pecans, then add this to the creamed mixture.
  5. Now mix the batter until it becomes a kneadable dough.
  6. Put teaspoonfulls of dough 2 inches apart onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350° for 8 minutes. Remove from cookie sheets, and cool on wire racks.
