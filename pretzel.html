Pretzel Bites

Prep time: 40 mins

Yield: 32

INGREDIENTS:










STEPS:
  1. Quarter each biscuit, then roll each piece into a ball. Using a knife, carve an X into each ball.
  2. In a saucepan, combine water and baking soda. Bring to boil then reduce to a simmer. Add balls and cook for 30 seconds. (The dough will float to top.) Remove with a slotted spoon and transfer to baking sheet, making sure the pretzels aren't touching.
  3. Brush beaten egg over each pretzel, then sprinkle with coarse salt.
  4. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Meanwhile, make a cheese dipping sauce: Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute. Stir in milk and bring to a simmer. When milk begins to thicken, stir in cheese, whisking constantly until sauce is smooth. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and cayenne. Serve with the pretzels.
